# Content System

## Format

Hook → Problem → Insight → CTA

---

## Hook Examples

- If your credit score is under 600, stop doing this
- Nobody tells you this about bad credit
- This is why your score is not improving
- You can check this without hurting your credit

---

Check the link in bio to see your options

Scale:
- 20+ posts per day

Find high-performing content and repeat it
